Menu

This Season

A seven-course progression.

Our menu changes with the harvest and the catch. The current progression moves from raw and bright through fire and richness, finishing on the garden. Dietary needs are easily accommodated with a day's notice. Let us know when you book.

The Courses

  1. Oyster & Apple Day-boat oyster, green apple, frozen horseradish
  2. Garden Crudité Raw and pickled vegetables, herb emulsion
  3. Hand-Cut Pasta Brown butter, aged parmesan, black pepper
  4. Line-Caught Fish Brassica leaves, roasted squash, shellfish jus
  5. Aged Duck Pommes purée, confit potato, peppercorn sauce
  6. Cheese & Honey Local cheese, comb honey, walnut
  7. Raspberry & Cream Set sheep's-milk cream, toasted crumble, garden herbs
