The Table

Cooking that follows the season, plate by plate.

Pepper is a contemporary dining room where the menu changes with what the farms and coast send in. Each evening is built around a single tasting progression, refined, generous, and quietly precise. We keep the room small so the kitchen can stay close to every plate that leaves it.

What to Expect

Three ways to spend the evening

  • Featured Dish

    Raspberry & Sheep's Milk

    A delicate set cream with fresh raspberry, toasted crumble, and garden herbs. Our signature close to the tasting menu.

  • Tasting Menu

    Seven Courses

    A guided progression from the kitchen, paired course by course with an optional flight chosen by our sommelier.

  • Private Dining

    The Back Room

    An intimate space for celebrations and client dinners, with a menu shaped to the occasion and the season.

Reservations

Tables open thirty days out.

Service runs Tuesday through Saturday. We recommend booking early for weekends and special occasions. The dining room seats just forty.
