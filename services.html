Hospitality

Services

Beyond the Table

However you'd like to spend the evening.

From quiet celebrations to client dinners, our team can shape the experience around you. Reach out and we'll handle the details before you arrive.

What We Offer

  • Private Dining Evening Service Our back room seats up to fourteen for a dedicated menu and your own service team.
  • Corporate Events By Appointment Full or partial buyouts for client dinners and team gatherings, with bespoke menus and pairings.
  • Concierge Services Daily 12 PM – 10 PM Help with special occasions, dietary planning, and requests from florals to celebration cakes.
  • Transportation Assistance Evening Hours We can arrange car service to and from the restaurant for private bookings on request.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Is there a dress code?

We suggest smart casual. There is no jacket requirement, but most guests dress up a little for the occasion.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. With a day's notice we can adapt the tasting menu for most allergies and dietary needs. Just note them when you reserve.

Do you host large parties?

The main dining room seats forty. For groups larger than fourteen we recommend a private buyout, which our team can arrange by appointment.

Is parking available?

Valet is available on weekend evenings. A public garage sits one block north on Harbor Lane.
