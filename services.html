Hospitality

Services

Beyond the Table

However you'd like to spend the evening.

From quiet celebrations to client dinners, our team can shape the experience around you. Reach out and we'll handle the details before you arrive.

What We Offer

  1. Private Dining

    Our back room seats up to fourteen for a dedicated menu and your own service team.

    • Evening service: Tuesday to Saturday, 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM
    • Menu tastings: Wednesday & Thursday, 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM (by appointment)
    • Final bookings: up to 72 hours before your date

  2. Corporate Events

    Full or partial buyouts for client dinners and team gatherings, with bespoke menus and pairings.

    • Planning consultations: Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
    • Private buyouts: Sunday & Monday, full evening availability
    • Group size: 15 to 40 guests

  3. Concierge Services

    Help with special occasions, dietary planning, and requests from florals to celebration cakes.

    • Concierge desk: Daily, 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM
    • Occasion requests: 48 hours' notice preferred
    • Dietary planning: any time before your service

  4. Transportation Assistance

    We can arrange car service to and from the restaurant for private bookings on request.

    • Car service: Tuesday to Saturday, 4:00 PM to 11:00 PM
    • Valet parking: Friday & Saturday, 5:00 PM to 11:00 PM
    • Requests: arranged at booking or 24 hours ahead

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Is there a dress code?

We suggest smart casual. There is no jacket requirement, but most guests dress up a little for the occasion.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. With a day's notice we can adapt the tasting menu for most allergies and dietary needs. Just note them when you reserve.

Do you host large parties?

The main dining room seats forty. For groups larger than fourteen we recommend a private buyout, which our team can arrange by appointment.

Is parking available?

Valet is available on weekend evenings. A public garage sits one block north on Harbor Lane.
